About ProtestFind

Here at ProtestFind, you can find or create protests around the country regarding any type of social change.

In the wake of the death of George Floyd, the United States faces the incarnation of a civil rights movement, hundreds of years in the making.

Hence, it has become important more than ever for change. Change not only in civil rights, but change in all aspects of society. This is the inspiration for ProtestFind.

  • ProtestFind is an online service that allows people to create and join protests throughout the country.

    This helps people organize and communicate easier with regards to holding rallies or protests.

  • Once your account is created, you are able to create and join protests.

    You may create a new protest by providing a location or join a protest by finding a location near you and mark yourself as going.

F.A.Q

Frequently Asked Questions

  • How do I create a protest?

    To create a protest, you first must have an account with ProtestFind. If you do not have an account, navigate to the Register page and create an account, then proceed to logging. If you already have an account, you may simply log in. Then navigate to the Create Protest page by clicking into the Protest dropdown. You may then input all information about the protest and create the protest once you are finished. Ta Da! Your protest has been created!

  • How do I join a protest?

    To join a protest, you first must have an account with ProtestFind. If you do not have an account, navigate to the Register page and create an account, then proceed to logging. If you already have an account, you may simply log in. Then navigate to the Find Protests page by clicking into the Protest dropdown. You may then find a protest you want to join, and click the Going! button. Ta Da! You have joined a protest!

  • How do I view a protest?

    To view a protest, navigate to the Find Protests page by clicking into the Protest dropdown. Then, select on the title of a protest you want to view. Once clicked, you will be able to see all the information about the protest as well as an embedded map via Google Maps API that shows the exact location of the event.

  • How can I update my account information?

    To change your account info, you must have an account and be logged in. Once you are logged in, navigate to the Account page. There, you can change your email and username. Once you have changed your username and/or your email, click the update button to save your information.

  • How can I update my profile picture?

    To change your profile picture, you must have an account and be logged in. Once you are logged in, navigate to the Account page. There, you can click the choose file button and upload a file from your computer to be your new picture. Once you have changed your profile picture, click the update button to save your new picture.
