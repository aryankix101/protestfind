About RallyConnect

Here at RallyConnect, you can find or create any protest, rally, or march around the country regarding any type of social change and awareness that speaks to you.

F.A.Q

Frequently Asked Questions

  • How do I create a rally?

    To create a rally, you first must have an account with RallyConnect. If you do not have an account, navigate to the Register page and create an account, then proceed to log in. If you already have an account, you may simply log in. Then navigate to the Create Rally page by clicking into the Rallies dropdown. You may then input all information about the rally and create the rally once you are finished. Ta Da! Your rally has been created!

  • How do I join a rally?

    To join a rally, you first must have an account with RallyConnect. If you do not have an account, navigate to the Register page and create an account, then proceed to log in. If you already have an account, you may simply log in. Then navigate to the Find Rallies page by clicking into the Rallies dropdown. You may then find a rally you want to join, and click the Going! button. Ta Da! You have joined a rally!

  • How do I view a rally?

    To view a rally, navigate to the Find Rallies page by clicking into the Rallies dropdown. Then, select on the title of a rally you want to view. Once clicked, you will be able to see all the information about the rally as well as an embedded map via Google Maps API that shows the exact location of the event.

  • How can I update my account information?

    To change your account info, you must have an account and be logged in. Once you are logged in, navigate to the Account page. There, you can change your email and username. Once you have changed your username and/or your email, click the update button to save your information.

  • How can I update my profile picture?

    To change your profile picture, you must have an account and be logged in. Once you are logged in, navigate to the Account page. There, you can click the choose file button and upload a file from your computer to be your new picture. Once you have changed your profile picture, click the update button to save your new picture.
